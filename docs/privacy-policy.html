Privacy Policy

Solitaire (com.paultranvan.solitaire) · Effective date: 4 June 2026

Solitaire is a single-player card game developed by Paul Tran-Van ("we", "us"). We designed it to respect your privacy completely: the app does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data.

Information we collect

None. The app has no accounts, no login, and no servers. We do not ask for, gather, or receive any personal or identifying information about you.

Data stored on your device

To make the game work, the app saves the following locally on your device only, using your device's private app storage:

  • Your current game in progress, so you can resume it later.
  • Your game statistics and records (wins, times, scores).
  • Your preferences (such as draw count, language, and sound settings).

This information never leaves your device. It is not uploaded anywhere, and we cannot see it. Uninstalling the app, or clearing the app's data in your device settings, permanently deletes all of it.

Network and tracking

The app works fully offline. It contains no analytics, no advertising, no tracking or fingerprinting technologies, and no third-party SDKs that collect data. It does not request access to your location, contacts, camera, microphone, photos, or files.

Permissions

The app may use device vibration (haptic feedback) to enhance gameplay. This does not involve collecting any data.

Children's privacy

The app is suitable for all ages. Because it collects no data from anyone, it collects no data from children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this same address with a new effective date.
